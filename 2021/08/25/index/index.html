About me

About me

I am a third year PhD student in the Computer Science and Engineering Department of University of Notre Dame. My advisor is Dr. Yiyu Shi. Here is our lab website SCL. Currently, my research focuses on Deep Learning for Medical Image Applications, and Self-supervised Learning. Before coming to University of Notre Dame, I received my master’s and bachelor’s degrees at Huazhong University of Science & Technology advised by Dr. Yu Hu.
